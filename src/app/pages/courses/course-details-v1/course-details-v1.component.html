This course is designed to cater to all levels of learners. Whether you're completely new to Angular or have experience with earlier versions like Angular 1 or Angular 2, you're in the right place.

From the initial setup to the deployment of your applications, this comprehensive course covers every aspect. You'll dive deep into topics such as Components, Directives, Services, Forms, HTTP Access, Authentication, and optimizing your Angular applications using Modules and Offline Compilation.
